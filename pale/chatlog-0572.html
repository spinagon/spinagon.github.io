JayManiac 11/25/2022 4:41 PM
My current understanding, just to get it out of the way so it doesn't bother me, is that City Magic and Paths Magic are perfect opposites. And not opposites in the way that goblins and faeries are opposites where they're two sides of the same coin and there's animosity to the way they interact, no, more like metaphysical opposites. They're each locations between fundamental forces we might call Firmament (Earth, Reality, Humanity) and Void (Nothing) and they're locations that exist because of each other. The Paths are the bits of Firmament that are close to the Void and Cityspace/City Magic is made up of the bit of Void that gets into the Firmament. (edited)
4:45 PM
That's not a perfect framing of it, but it's a theoretical framework that I find kind of useful. And it's why the most effective magic on the Paths besides Path magic itself seems to be that which we might associate with Firmament (the Sharonballs, Founding). It's also why Sootsleeves can 'update' herself with City Magic, because she's an idea-fragment with edges that act like a sort of 'universal adapter' to a lot of Firmament-adjacent magic. (edited)
4:47 PM
Or put another way, there are shards of Void in City Magic, spaces of Nothing that make it all work and flow, and Sootsleeves can easily slot herself into that to tap into those flows.
