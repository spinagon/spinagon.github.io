They see the cause/effect of it, but it doesn't click. A NT kid might see that X thing happened because of Y action, but someone with ED might come to the conclusion that X thing happened because parent found out about Y action, for example. This disconnect between how the caregiver is looking at the situation and how the kid is seeing it can lead to...well, the kid not 'answering the question as intended', to put it in the terms of the bio test. They might figure out the system and try to perform well enough that they avoid the consequences. This link is because the school system is practically a codification of parenting tactics that sure as fuck don't work for everyone.
