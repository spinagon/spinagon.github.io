It depends a lot on your writing process. I'm not altogether satisfied with it, but as it stands Scrivener is how I organize a lot of my ideas and planning, so I typically have a doc open most of the time in case I need to jot down thoughts I have while reading or working. I don't typically use it for long-form writing but when I have it's been very functional.
