I've said it before and I'll say it again with regard to colonialism: just because our imperialist concepts can be applied to a thing, does not mean they are the thing. For example: Religion was a term created by Christians in England that mostly referred to which sect of Christianity you were following (as it came into use around the time of the protestant reformation) and then within the century was turned on the rest of the world as imperialism started happening. We can look at the beliefs of indigenous peoples and call them religions and not be entirely wrong, but that does not mean they were religions before we called them that.
