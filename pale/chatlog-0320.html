Also, my theory on what Undercities are: It seems to me they get a lot of the personal impulses that are suppressed up above, right? Things that are socially unacceptable, to whatever degree that applies. This means that most Undercities will end up being violent, because suppressing violent impulses is something everyone everywhere does to one degree or another. So that's not the interesting part. The interesting part is everything else. I think you can learn a lot of an upper city by seeing what flourishes in its Undercity. Extremely homophobic places will likely have an LGBTQ scene in their Undercities, for example. And Vice Kennet, to Verona at least, is a place that's more alive, but that, especially, allows creativity to flourish, in graffiti art and even Mal the tattoo artist. Which further emphasises that Kennet is just... kind of a dull place that doesn't really give that creativity an outlet, doesn't support the kind of people who would want to do it. (edited)
