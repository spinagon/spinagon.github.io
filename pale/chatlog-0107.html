Assume, unless stated otherwise, that the Demesne is a challenge to penetrate. Even a greater practitioner may find it difficult or impossible to intrude if a practitioner of the lowest tier is within. The space is the practitioner’s in every respect, with permission to enter contingent on their permission. Within, matter may be shaped, power transformed or put to efficient use, and spaces demarcated for specific uses. The practitioner can be considered an unchallenged lesser deity within their place of power, with control over contents, rules, and boundaries within the space, although the strictures and nature of the original space will impact the limits and bounds, as will the nature of the practitioner themselves.This is where the Question I opened with really comes to bear, in that last part about the strictures and nature of the original space. We get a lot of versatility with what you can do within your demesne, so I feel as if the nature of the original space has been somewhat more overlooked in our discussions on the matter. I mean, compared to the Implement, the symbology of a place would seem to have a lot less relevance to its use within the Practice for whatever reason? Its influence is a footnote, not the main thesis. And while it seems much more focused predictions about a practitioner can be drawn from their choice of implement, the emphasized part of the demesne is its versatility time and time again.
7:40 PM
These limitations are flexible ones, and investment of time, attention, and power will allow a practitioner to grow the effective space or challenge its rules more and more with time. This grows more difficult as the limits are tested, and thus an expenditure of power might expand a space by twenty percent with the first instance, fifteen with the next, and ten the next, diminishing each time until it is fruitless. This expenditure is easy and natural, and may be directed at other functions, aspects of the space, or qualities of the space, though the nature of the original space will constrain all things, not only dimension.I suppose this is because of the demesne's place within our world...where the implement is a part of you expressed in the language of the greater universe- as it essentially defines not the Self, but the way in which the Self interacts with reality -the demesne is an expression of the Self, to the Self. By controlling what aspects of the world enter our demesne even down to the spirits themselves we can essentially write our own language with which to work. We determine the rules within our space, and can bend and break them much more easily because this is not an activity focused upon one's interaction with the world at large.
7:44 PM
Note, however, that depending on how the Demesne is cultivated, this personal place may develop in a variety of ways. Virtually every statement and rule listed within this text may be challenged, but one should expect that this challenge has its own cost and weakens the end result. Because the bones of the demesne ritual are so malleable, we draw on a variety of examples to illustrate, all real-life cases, some with names changed and details slightly altered.Which is something that I think says a great deal. That while your influence on the world is on its own terms, you can make an effort to break the rules that make it up whilst in the comfort of your home. It still comes at the cost of power, since you cannot escape being part of the world and the society in which you were raised and in which you exist, but...it's a part of the practice that can be suited to the individual if the individual lets it, if that's where their priorities are. And that's a surprising note of hope in this system...even if it is based around the idea that you have to first claim this slice of reality through challenge and competition and capitalistic undertones.
