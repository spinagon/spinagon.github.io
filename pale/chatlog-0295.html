Fairly unresearched, as developmental disorders go. The dreamer in question will process things via repetative movement and stimming during which they brainstorm and conjure vividly real narratives, often for hours on end. I think that this is the bottom line when it comes to mental disorders in general. A disorder is a disorder because it's at odds with the society you're supposed to be functioning in, but while there is some grandiose society that we're all a part of, it's also important to take into account the individual situations that create disordered-ness.
