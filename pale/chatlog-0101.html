19/nb, Enchantress and Glamourist, seeks Other for Casual familiar bond --- In finding a familiar comfort, companionship, and cooperation are my priorities far above power or utility. Do we get along? Do we trust each other? Can we communicate effectively? Do we recharge each other's identities by our cohabitation, or wear on them? I wish only that we may each have our own goals, our own circles and our own freedoms, without it weighing on our mutual trust in the bond and in each other; I understand that this kind of relationship is one to be cultivated, rather than found. Ideally, our relationship would be defined by us, rather than by the systems which govern the world. I am young, and my identity may be subject to change in the future; I have attempted to distill what I see as constants of my identity here, but any aspiring familiar of mine must be aware that I am an ever-shifting being, and by joining me, you would be along for wherever that ride takes us. As your familiar I would offer creativity, fluidity, and humanity. I have a knack for connections, for bringing people together and acting as pillars of understanding between them; I often forge connections quickly and easily without their strength suffering for it, and will typically move between several groups of friends- Other and Human alike -fostering companionship and creating comfortable roles throughout. If you are someone who struggles to network with humanity and with Others, I could provide you a place within these systems, and a voice amongst their people.

Perhaps the most valuable thing I've to offer those in my circles is freedom. I See easily the ties that bind, and feel an easiy intiuation for any such abstract aspects of the Practice. For those in my close circles I'm adept at finding ways to shirk these burdens, shape their nature with language and leave meaningful traces of that influence long after I am gone. In keeping with people and Others, it seems reality finds me persuasive. If you are less than pleased with the space you now inhabit, your current limitations and restrictive nature, I can work with you to change it, become a version of you that you're more content with. I seek to change the system, the Seal, the way things are and the way they should be. I shout such changes to the world, hoping perhaps in vain that they will accomplish something, but really, they start with you. With those I'm closest to. Many practitioners offer a break, from your nature as an Other. I would offer to break you from it in small ways, ways that I hope can persist even after I am gone. As a practitioner I am not especially lacking in power, so power is not an enormous requirement. To the ends of freedom and pushing the boundaries, I am entirely open to Anti-Establishment Others, and believe in my ability to find our own equilibrium despite the foundations of the system. I will warn that these traits can be read as symptoms of my arrogance, which you may find it prudent to account for.
