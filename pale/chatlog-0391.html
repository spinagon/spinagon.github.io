Year Zero, Day Zero: What is the use of a sanctuary without protection? What is the use of a sisterhood without love? What is the use of practice if I cannot do anything in the face of the greatest pain I have ever felt? Minnie my daughter was dead and the reasons for her death were already fiction before the dirt had been thrown over her. The church supports that fiction for I have told them the truth yet I was casually dismissed and urged to return home. The coven who taught me the practice had no succor to offer me. The practice, scattered hedge magics taught to me by local women, cannot bring my child back to me.And we don't even start on the Paths, we start with a reflection on the gendered politics of practice and nonpractice here, and perhaps more importantly for the purposes of this text and its significance: someone who's very articulate and who sorts out her thoughts through writing. Her journalistic style and the way she reflects on the events of her life just immediately hits us with that baseline of personality, conviction, and purpose. As a meta-choice it's fantastic, because it's easy to imagine a Hazel who's just sort of dragged around by the story Alice-style and doesn't get a lot of active observation or agency in the narrative, but this immediately subverts that with a picture of Hazel as someone with a lot of drive and a very strong personal orientation.
